Translation Notes

[^]: Dead end land + mention mayoi CG + mention who was in it
[^]: Reverse live TL note about hyori chiaki rivalry
[^]: Referring to Meteor Impact Chapter 8 part 2, when Madara was selling grilled onigiri in front of the school gate.
[^]: Sutras are Buddhist scriptures. It's a custom for buddhists to chant them.
[^]: that dame kana tl note from sweet halloween えっ。普通に怖い!
幻聴ということにして気付かなかったことにしたいが…・・・見に行かなきゃ駄目かなあっ!? also read sweet halloween
[^]: Chiaki switches from his usual way of speaking and speaks in polite language here (keigo). He's said something like this before in Meteor Impact Chapter 1 part 1
[^]: “Side dish” is okazu (おかず) in Japanese, referring to the supplementary dish that's eaten along with the staple food (typically cooked rice).
[^]: Mitsuru's favorite food. See here: https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hamburg_steak
[^]: Mitsuru says oishiku naare, a very common Japanese phrase said when cooking or serving food, in hopes of it turning out tasty for the one who'll eat it. It's also a type of fanservice found in maid cafes and the like.
[^]: See here for risotto https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Risotto
[^]: The quote is ricchan ganbatta ne, which sounds a little more like how a kid speaks. It's different from how Mao typically speaks (Mao would say ganbatta na, which sounds more rough/masculine).
[^]: See here for paella https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Paella
[^]: Nice-P sometimes uses English words, written in roman letters. I kept it as is and added italics to make it clear it's said in English in the original text.
[^]: See here for curry bread https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Curry_bread
