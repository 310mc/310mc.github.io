Translation Notes

  1. ↑ This tale is shown in Ninja Clan, the story connected to this song.
  2. ↑ The “likes/loves” mentioned with quotation marks refers to the thing(s) they love. In Japanese, the word is suki or daisuki. Ryuseitai’s “likes” is a running theme in their story, songs, as well as their solo songs. In particular, Tropical and its song RELAX PARADISE discuss it.
  3. ↑ Referring to the main story, when Chiaki and Kanata fire off a Ryuseitai move.
  4. ↑ These are the mascot characters that appeared in Tropical.
  5. ↑ The fish song is performed in Stella Maris, and Midori goes through quite the experience while practicing. Please give the story a read to find out what happens!
  6. ↑ After the event “Universe” (i.e. Supervillain) ended, “Ryuseitai Power Up”, the all-leaders Ryuseitai, was introduced. You can see their introduction video here, and compare the changes with their previous introduction video from 2019.
