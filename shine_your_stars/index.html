Nevertheless, if there are even just a few people out there who grew to love ninjas because of me, I could not be any happier~♪

It feels a little embarrassing to be complimented so directly, but I’m happy!

The first time we met a long time ago, you mistook me for a weird guy, but… You’re now the person who recognizes my actions the most.

That trust is the very source of my strength now! Thank you, Anzu! Let’s protect everyone’s smiles together from here on out, as well…☆

Yeah, thank you! Your thoughtful words are enough to blow away all my exhaustion!
