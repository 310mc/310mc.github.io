Anzu-dono! Did you find the magazine you were looking for?

I am glad to hear it, then! I also successfully acquired the magazine featuring ninjas~♪

However, I could have never expected them to be featured in a regular entertainment magazine, instead of a history-based one… This was quite the blind spot for me.

I’m so glad you managed to find it.

If memory serves me right, this is the very first time this magazine features ninjas, so it was a pleasant surprise…!

Eh? My idol activities have been a contributing factor to spreading the charm of ninjas?

Yaaay, I’m so happy to hear that~♪ Just those words alone give me the strength to work harder and harder~♪

I wonder if ninjas have become popular now?

Perhaps my hard work has shown fruition, and there are now an increasing number of people who realize the charm of ninjas—

No, that’s a little too self-conceited.

Nevertheless, if there are even a few people out there who grew to love ninjas because of me, I could never be any happier~♪
