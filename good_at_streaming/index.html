This story is set a little after the Mega Sphere arc began (July), where idols start live-streaming through Mega Stream.

Season: ES 2nd Year, Summer

(Sigh, I’m exhausted… I wonder if the bakery below is still selling croissants at this hour…)1

(Focused or not, working endlessly without eating breakfast or lunch would undoubtedly wear anyone out, including myself.)

(Perhaps I shouldn’t have held back from asking Kagehira to fetch me some lunch after all.)

(Oh? The person over there…)

MEGA LIVE on @Nagumo_Ryusei

“Aight, it’s time I end the live stream. Thanks for joinin’, everyone~”

(Ah, Nagumo. It appears he was live-streaming here. Good grief, he truly works diligently, doesn’t he.)

(…The camera may catch sight of me from the corners if I stand here. I’ll head off promptly.)

(Oh? Nagumo’s jacket…)

Whew, that’s that for the live stream. I feel thirsty after talkin’ so much.

Nagumo, a word with you, if you may? The thread of your jacket’s hem has come undone.

Eh!? Woah, you’re right!? Don’t tell me it was also like this while I was streaming!?

Uwah~ That’s kinda embarrassin’. I guess nobody pointed it out for my sake…

No, it isn’t possible to tell at a simple glance, so I assume they hadn’t noticed at all.

Itsuki-senpai, thank ya for lettin’ me know. You helped me out!

But that all bein’ said… I was plannin’ on wearin’ these training clothes for other stuff too, so this is bad…

Well, I guess I can fix this much on my own… Maybe? I’ll try at least.

…You’ve left me no choice.

Eh?

I mean to say that I will mend it for you. Stand over there, quickly.

Eh!? Are you sure it’s okay?

I always carry with me a basic sewing kit, so I can mend a simple loosened hem.

Right, do not move an inch. You could risk getting pricked by the needle otherwise.

Several minutes later…

Ooh~! So quick! So clean! You stitched it up like a pro!

Non! How insolent of you! I am already working as a professional artist!

Ah, sorry! But I just meant that it moved me that much!

I knew from Taishou that you’re good at sewin’, but like, Itsuki-senpai’s level of amazingness is even more than I imagined!

That all being said and done, what on earth have you been doing for your clothes to get this tattered? I found plenty of other threads that were undone, you know.

Uu~myu… Oh yeah, I think my clothes got caught in some ornament when I was practicin’ outside yesterday.

Good grief… Well, what’s done is done, I suppose.

I was on my way to the bakery, so I shall take my leave here.

Ossu! Thank you very much!

Ah—!

You’re truly noisy. What is it this time?

‘Scuse me, Itsuki-senpai, I’m sorry ‘bout this! I thought I’d turned off the live stream’s camera, but it looks like it didn’t actually turn off…

So everythin’ we were talkin’ about got streamed to the whole world…!

What…!?

Woah, woah, there’s so many comments comin’ in! “Wow, amazing!” “I’m so jealous of how skilled he is!” “Itsuki-kun is so kind”…

My fans are goin’ crazy! There’s even comments askin’, “What will you buy at the bakery?”

What does it matter what I’ll buy!?

(How could this happen…! I knew that there were cameras in this room as well, but I never expected it would cause this much of a ruckus…)

Sound of footsteps

Itsuki! Thank you for taking care of our Nagumo!

Eh!? Morisawa-senpai, why’re you here!?

I was watching over Nagumo’s stream in our room and then caught sight of his conversation with Itsuki!2 Your conversation moved me so much that I just had to rush over here!

Thank you, Itsuki! Your sewing skills are truly reassuring to have!

My hand hurts! Your handshakes are far too strong!

Uwah!? The comments are goin’ even crazier now!

“It’s Chiaki-kun!” “Itsuki-kun’s amazing”… There’s so many comments pourin’ in that I can barely keep up!

Itsuki-senpai! Next time around, I’d love you to teach me and everyone else how to sew!

Good grief…

It’s because these sorts of incidents can occur that I cannot grow fond of streaming, truly.
