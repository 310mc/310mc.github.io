Scurrying 1

Season: ES 2nd Year, Summer (2nd Week of July)

The next day…

everyone in ryuseitai~ great job at coming on [time]~♪

thank you for coming to my [declaration of conviction] today~♪

Ah, sure. We gathered at the stadium like you asked, but… What’s goin’ on? We live in the same room, so we could’ve just got together there, right?

tsk, tsk, tsk…♪ a [declaration of conviction] must be said in front of a [camera], you know?

if i just say it [in secret] in my [room], nobody will be able to know.

Hm. Shinkai-dono typically takes it easy, so if you’re taking action on your own accord, it must be serious.

Yeah. It’s like, how do I put it… He has a different expression in his eyes… I can feel his determination.

Tell us, Kanata. What sort of declaration of conviction do you intend to speak out?

yes, i will say the [conclusion].

i intend to become one of the [six of song].

mm, no. i will become one.

Shinkai-dono, becoming one of the Six of Song…? Those eyes look absolutely legit.

Kanata, your determination was heard loud and clear.

…However, why did you decide to declare it all of a sudden? If you were aiming to be one of the Six of Song, there were plenty of other timings you could’ve mentioned it.

there comes a [time] when a man must fight.

i thought that by declaring my conviction here, it shows that i am cutting off all [retreats].

Ooh, that’s so manly of ya, Shinkai-senpai!

You really are that determined… Your determination to be one of the Six of Song is totally getting through to everyone in this worldwide stream!

This isn’t somethin’ anyone can do, y’know!

fufufu. tetora is also cool, being a man of [action before words].

but i will only cause [misunderstandings] if i do not explain myself, so i will at least mention my reason.

i want to save my [friend].

Friend…?

yes. shu is [pushing] himself too much.

he is being caught in the flow of the [situation], just like in the past… i cannot stand to watch.

so i plan to become one of the [six of song], and create a [stir] in what is happening.

I see. So it’s about Itsuki… I assume this means you still have some regrets from the time when you were one of the Five Eccentrics.

I’ll support you then, Kanata.

Actually, it isn’t only Kanata. I’m supporting everyone in Ryuseitai.

I was chosen as one of the Six of Song in Sailing Ceremony, so I was hoping everyone else would get chosen, too!

And here you are, Kanata, letting us know your feelings. So I’ll be sure to back you up with all I’ve got!

Mhn, I share the same feelings~♪

I’m happy no matter who gets chosen from Ryuseitai. If there’s something I can assist with, do let me know!

Everyone’s so proactive… Well, it’s rare to see Shinkai-senpai this motivated, and if that’s how determined he is, then I’ll help out a little, too.

You’ll fight for the sake of your friend… That’s Shinkai-senpai’s manly courage showing!

But if we had to compete with each other in the end, make sure not to hold back, ‘kay?

If we just hand over the win to Shinkai-senpai, it wouldn’t be a fair and square match.

yes, of course. let’s not [hold back] if that moment comes.

Fufu, you’ve got a good look in your eyes, Kanata.

The only thing that worries me is that Kanata doesn’t really excel at all in races.

I don’t believe you can compete with the usual way you go about things, so do you have any sort of plan?

no. i plan to pour my [all] even if i cannot win. because i will be [competing] over the amount of [nice!]’s—

well then, it is time i go…♪

Uoooh, such a cool-lookin’ back…! I can totally feel Shinkai-senpai’s spirit!

But it was still a really sluggish way to walk… Will he really be alright?

Mfun! Shinkai-dono accomplishes his duty when it’s time! I’m certain he will take his declaration into heart, and show us all how cool he is…♪
