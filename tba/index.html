An Exhibition Beyond Expectations

Chiaki is excitedly enjoying an exhibition full of sentai hero displays along with Kaoru, when suddenly, Yume appears…

Season: ES 2nd Year, Summer (June)

Location: Department Store (Sentai Hero Exhibition)

UOOOHH! I never thought I’d ever get to see the hero outfits of current broadcasts this close-up…!

There’s miniature figurines of all the past kaijuu lined up over there. And even dioramas that showcase the place that the hero and kaijuu fought!

Since I can’t take photos here, I have to be sure to sear every little thing into my mind! ♪

Ahaha. You look like you’re having fun, Moricchi.

Mm, sorry… I got all excited on my own.

Nah, don’t sweat it.

You were looking forward to this sentai hero exhibition, weren’t you? Don’t worry about me, enjoy it as much as you like.

No, I can’t let myself do that!

Sudden invitation or not, you came here with me after all, Hakaaze.

If you hadn’t joined me, I would’ve been all in my lonesome checking out the exhibition today.

That’s exactly why I hope to make you enjoy this event too, Hakaze…!

You’re exaggerating things… I just didn’t have anything scheduled today, so I came with you to kill time, that’s all.

I don’t know sentai heroes as well as you do, so sadly I can’t keep up with your talks, but…

I’ll be enjoying myself here in my own way, so really, don’t worry about me.

That’s good to know then.

…Aaalright! If there’s anything you don’t know, feel free to ask me! I’ll make sure to explain it in digestible terms so that you can also enjoy it! ☆

Ahaha. I’ll rely on you once the moment comes.

That all being said, the scale of this exhibition is way bigger than I thought it’d be. I heard it’s an exhibition aimed at kids, so I’m a little surprised.

Yeah, I did hear that it’s a big museum that reserved a whole event space in a department store, but I’m just as surprised as you that they put this much effort in it.

I expected there to be a lot of parents with their kids, but it looks like there’s a pretty hefty amount of people older than us here too.

This exhibition doesn’t only display sentai heroes of current shows, but also sentai heroes from past shows, that’s why.

There’s heroes displayed here that would also feel nostalgic to people much older than us, people from the same generation as our parents, even.

Ohh. That’s why I see some heroes I’m familiar with.

…Huh? That area over there seems different in its air…?

Mm? …Ahh, that’s where they’re displaying all the magical girls, aimed towards little girls.

This exhibition is a joint display with shows that broadcast on Sunday mornings, along with Super Hero Time shows.

Magical girls, huh~ Do you watch those too, Moricchi?

Since the shows get broadcasted alongside sentai hero shows, there’s plenty of times I watch those, too.

Huh… That’s a world I barely really know, honestly~

I know my older sister used to watch them when we were young, but I don’t know their names or how they look much.

That’s pretty normal when it’s not part of your interest. I’m not all that well-versed in them, either.

Still, they’re having a display of everything and all, so it might be nice to peek into it a little.

Ehh~? Won’t we look like we’re in the wrong place…?

Haha, you’re overthinking it. Look, there’s a bunch of guys around our age over there, too.

Oh, you’re right. I wonder if they’re accompanying their little sister or something?

Hm, it looks like this area is displaying all the outfits and life-sized figures of magical girls.

I can tell the craftsman was very particular with every detail, from the fingertips to the silhouette of the hair. Such wonderful art…☆

Fufun. Looks like there’s someone who gets it a little, hm?

(...Mm? This kid suddenly spoke to us… I think I’ve seen them somewhere before…?)

Are you Yume-kun, by any chance?

Yume…? Ah~ As in, the new idol that recently joined ES? He’s in NewDi, right?

Yeah. He’s part of the unit Esupuri.

Yume knows about you. Yume sometimes sees you with Midori-sama, after all.

Ahh, I’m so happy that you know about me! ♪ Please do get along well with Takamine! ☆

But I never expected us to meet in a place like this. Have you also come here because you like this craft, Yume-kun?

Yes. Yume was looking forward to it for a loooong time.

The craft is obviously something Yume was excited for, but Yume would even check the social media account of this figurine’s modeler.

(Whispering) Moricchi, what’s a modeler?

(Whispering) It’s referring to the craftsman behind making this figurine. Yume-kun must be a fan of them.

A big exhibition for this sort of creative work is a rare sight in itself.

Yume was always planning to visit/come here, but after hearing that the one in charge of the exhibition is someone who likes the modeling of this figurine, Yume couldn’t help but be very excited to see it.

It’s as you mention; from the fingertips to the volume of the hair, and even the balance of the figurine… There’s only one modeler out there who would have this much incredible understanding of the character.

Yume is in love no matter how many times Yume looks at it. This little girl looks happy about getting to look so cute, too. ♪

Fufu. I understand, Yume-kun.

If I learned that a suit actor I respect is participating in my beloved hero shows, I would’ve also been all the more excited to see that performance.

I can tell just by how much you’re praising it, Yume-kun. It must’ve been a very particular choice by the modeler to have deliberately modelled it in a different pose from her famous one.

…! Yes, exactly that…!

The average person immediately jumps at whatever is recognizable, so they get snappy and say, “Why didn’t they model this figurine with the most famous pose you see on posters?”...

But when making a 3D model of something, the right way to do it is to make it the pose of her first appearance.

Still, the modeler was likely unsure at first, too. However, seeing how high quality it turned out, they surely didn’t make the wrong choices.

But of course, it would’ve still turned out to be a wonderful craft even with another pose.

Yume also agrees. But that is exactly why this little girl, who got crafted into this specific pose, is so valuable.

Mhm! The troubles they went through are exactly why the final product has so much value to it.

But now I’m certain there’s particular focus points of the rest of the figurines, too. It frustrates me that I can’t spot them due to my lack of knowledge.

Ufufu, then Yume will tell you all about it. ♪

Are you sure? It looks like you came by yourself here, and I don’t want to disturb your time if you’d like to go around the place at your own pace…

Yume doesn’t mind. Any person who understands the intentions behind this pose is no bad person.

Yume will personally teach you all about it. ♪

Ahaha, that’s just wonderful! Don’t you think so, Hakaze!

Huh? Me?

Oh, I know! I came here to look at all the sentai heroes!

Maybe it doesn’t work as an exchange per se, but if you’re alright with it, how about you entrust me with guiding you around that, Yume-kun?

No, Yume isn’t really interested in sentai heroes.

…But, everyone has their own favorite thing.

Since you are someone who understands the beauty of this little girl, there may be value in hearing you out.

Yume looks forward to hearing interesting talks from you…♪

Yeah, you can count on it…☆ Alright, then let’s start with this figurine first!

(Moricchi looks to be having fun, huh~ I don’t think they’re gonna run out of topics anytime soon, so I don’t think they’ll notice me quietly leaving…?)

(Either way… It’s always nice to get to talk to one another passionately, just like those two are, isn’t it~? ♪)
