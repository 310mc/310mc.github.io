Sailing Ceremony – Chiaki Mini Talk: Happy News

Officially released on May 15, 2025 Enstars , 4. Character , Chiaki
2.3k ~3 min
Mini Talk
Point of View
Anzu
Translation
310mc
Story
Sailing Ceremony

1/3

Ohh, Anzu! Fancy meeting you here!

I’m happy to see you looking energetic today, too! Energy is number one when it comes to us people…☆

Oh yeah, there’s something I’d like you to see.

A pin badge?

Yep! This is a limited edition badge of one of my beloved heroes.

And would you believe it!? I found it in Mega Sphere’s general store!

It’s hard to find this sold in stores, so I had to show it off to someone. Fufu, it looks so cool no matter how many times I look at it~♪

That’s an action figure, right?

I went to the convenience store earlier, and pulled a raffle ticket of my beloved heroes!

I never expected Mega Sphere’s convenience stores to have one of these hero raffles, so I couldn’t help myself from pulling a few times!

And in doing so, I got to win the very last action figure you can only get once. Fufu, I’m on a roll today…♪

2/3

Mm? Is that why you’re in such a good mood?

Fufu, of course I’d be in this much of a good mood, y’know! ♪ I came across something I love in a place I never expected to find it in!

Come on, be happy together with me, Anzu…♪

I’m glad for you.

Yeah! I’ll display it in my own space of our room! ☆1

But honestly, I’d much rather display it somewhere more noticeable than my space in the room.

Actually, maybe I could line up all of Ryuseitai’s favorite things and display them together…♪

You did it!

Thank you, Anzu. I’m so happy to share my happiness with someone!

From what I’ve heard, Mega Sphere has basically everything, so the things you’d want should appear in the stores in no time, hopefully.

I assume you’re busy right now, but I recommend you check the Passage Arcade in-between your work.2

3/3

Huh, I see Kanata and Nagumo over on that side. I think they’re gesturing at us…

—Wait, that’s right! The five of us in Ryuseitai were going to have lunch together while planning our next live stream!

I got so excited over the merchandise that I completely forgot…! I have to go immediately!

Please enjoy yourself.

Mm! If we find a nice restaurant, I’ll let you know about it, Anzu!

You too, Anzu. You should take breaks without pushing yourself, okay? Make sure to stop after finishing a good chunk of your work. Promise me that!

I had fun talking! Right, farewell now…☆ (waves)

I’m looking forward to your live stream.

Yeah! Please do look forward to it! ☆

I heard Sengoku and Nagumo have been desperately trying to motivate Takamine for the live stream, so…

I’m sure you’ll get to see an absolutely flawless Ryuseitai on stream!

Translation Notes

  1. ↑ Chiaki is referring to the room he is living in with the rest of Ryuseitai over at Mega Sphere.
  2. ↑ This is the French word of passage. Passage refers to the shopping arcades that were built in Paris.
