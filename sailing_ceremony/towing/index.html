Context

  • After the idols entered Mega Sphere to check it out, Nice-P suddenly announces that they will be working at the Mega Sphere for one month, and cannot leave it until the end of the month.

  • Nice-P then kickstarts the Mega Stream, a month-long program where idols live stream themselves to gain Nice!’s. They are live-streamed 24/7 in public spaces, and they can also start their own live stream on their Mega Phone.

  • In Mega Sphere, units live in a room together. The unit rooms are not filmed for the live stream for privacy.

Chapter 1

Season: Summer (ES 2nd Year, July)

Next day. On the third day of Mega Stream…

“Our noon live stream ends here!”

“Come watch us tomorrow too, everyone! Ossu!”

fufu. today’s live stream went [swimmingly] too, didn’t it~ tetora?

you seem quite [at home] when it comes to [hosting] now, don’t you think?

No, no, I’m still nowhere near that level yet. I’ve only just gotten a handle of the live-streaming know-how.

What’s way more surprising is Midori-kun. He’s been talking ‘bout yuruchara and vegetables and the like… I never thought he’d have such a huge knack for live-streamin’.

Nah, I don’t really have a knack for it or anything…

I think it’s just that in a way, all five of us in Ryuseitai have different interests, so we can rake up a lot of ‘Nice!’s even when live-streaming solo, that’s all…

On top of that, we’ve been regularly doing collaboration live streams, so our viewership never grows stagnant.

The “#IWantToSeeRyuseitai’sSphereLive” campaign also came to our advantage, giving us a steady foundation… This is truly the fruit of our labor.

Well now. Seems like you Ryuseitai folks are enjoying yourselves with Mega Stream, huh?

Tori-kun… You were watching us?

You’re so insolent, commoner!

You should be humbling yourself before one of the Four Sky Kings! Show some respect!

Ha haa~!

Wh—He actually bowed his head down!? Why? Gross!

Fufu. Tori-kun’s attitude can be a little aggravating here and there, but if I just think of this as a historical drama, it actually feels quite pleasant! ♪

Yeah, he sounds like an evil magistrate — y’know, those baddies whose whole thing is to get defeated.

Hah!? You saying I’ll lose? As if!

Well, enjoy getting ahead of yourselves while you still can~♪

The Four Sky Kings’ power is on a monstrous level. Even if six opponents were against us, they’d have nooo chance of winning…☆

The rest of the kings haven’t been breaking a sweat one bit; they’ve just been taking it easy, ‘cause they’re saving their energy for the real thing!

Now he’s settin’ himself up even more as the ‘baddie who’s just gonna get defeated’…

Yeah, feels like this is foreshadowing him losing spectacularly…

this is what we call the ‘death flag’, isn’t it~?

Fits perfectly with “The Four Sky Kings Die” title-drop, indeed! ☆

Q-Quit talking with such insolence!

Wait, I don’t see Morisawa-senpai here — Where’s he gone to, anyway?

He’s the one with flying marks outside of the Four Sky Kings, right? So he’s been working alone without Ryuseitai…?

Ahh, that?

In regards to Morisawa-dono — He’s attempting to become a hero to rescue all the lost idols.

He has been performing collaboration live streams since yesterday, under the name of “Live Stream Supporting As I Please in Mega Stream”!

Live stream supporting as he pleases…!?

He’s not even the one managing the Mega Stream, so why the heck would he do that!? It literally makes no sense…!

Yeah, I thought the same at first, but…

It looks like Morisawa-senpai has pretty influential power right now, so…

Yup! Morisawa-senpai is currently the most popular idol in Mega Stream, right after the Four Sky Kings.

He’s so powerful right now, most idols would immediately grab onto the opportunity to collaborate with him if he said he’s interested.

So Morisawa-senpai proposed an idea to us, sayin’ he wants to utilize that influence to collab with all sortsa people.

That way, he may be able to rescue a certain person. That is what Morisawa-dono is aiming for! ♪

Rescue? What’s that about…?
