Context

Note: This is a partial translation of the story.

(You can also read the prologue for the context!)

  • After the idols entered Mega Sphere to check it out, NiceP suddenly announces that they will be working at the Mega Sphere for one month, and cannot leave it until the end of the month.

  • NiceP then kickstarts the Mega Stream, a month-long program where idols live stream themselves to gain Nice!’s. They are live-streamed 24/7 in public spaces, and they can also start their own live stream on their Mega Phone.

  • In Mega Sphere, units live in a room together. The unit rooms are not filmed for the live stream for privacy.
