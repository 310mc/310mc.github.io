Ensemble Stars!! Era Year 1 (ESY1)

The Ensemble Stars!! Era begins sometime in April, after the 3rd years graduated from Yumenosaki. Ryuseitai joins Starmaker Production after Eichi invites Chiaki to his agency.
Spring (April–May)
Stories marked with ★ are confirmed to be set in that month. Click Season Overview to learn more about this timeline.

The Ensemble Stars!! Era begins sometime in April, after the 3rd years graduated from Yumenosaki. Units from Yumenosaki join different agencies, and Ryuseitai joins Starmaker Production after Eichi invites Chiaki to his agency. Ryuseitai's Idol Story 1 illustrates their status during this time.

Tetora inherits the Ryusei Red leadership from Chiaki after the 3rd years graduated, making Tetora, Midori, and Shinobu the only seniors in the unit. Since Ryuseitai typically operates specifically in Yumenosaki, Tetora begins to accept Yumenosaki newcomers into the unit.

On the other hand, Ryuseitai also joined Starmaker Production with Chiaki and Kanata still part of the unit, so the unit also remained as the five of them when working under the agency.

To differentiate between these two versions of the unit, Ryuseitai decides to call the one under Tetora's leadership “Ryuseitai-N (N=Nagumo)” (operates mainly in Yumenosaki), and the one under Chiaki's leadership “Ryuseitai-M (M=Morisawa)” (operates mainly in Starmaker Production).
