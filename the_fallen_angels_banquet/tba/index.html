Chapter 1

Season: ES 2nd Year, Summer (Mid-June)

Several days later. Suou Family Residence, the corridors…

…Ohh. So this is Suou’s home~

I was already aware of this, but it really is completely different from my home. It’s like we’re on a set for a show.

I feel like Sengoku’d get super excited over a place like this.

…………

Woah, what’s wrong? You have a long face… Do you have a stomach ache or something?

No, not that…

I just remembered the time I got caught in a net over at Shinkai-dono’s home.

Got caught in a net? What on earth did you do to end up in such a situation?

Uu… My curiosity is the cause of it all.

Shinkai-dono’s home is also a remarkable mansion that oozes history, and it felt like I was in a ninja mansion.

So I got over-excited… as I tinkered about here and there, I ended up setting off the security traps…

A net fell from the ceiling and captured me, prohibiting me from entering.

I see, so that’s why you looked frightened.

Please don’t worry. There are no traps like that in my home.

However, there are many valuable items here, such as national treasure-ranked rice bowls as well as hanging scrolls that were passed down for generations.

We also have the finest selection of security guards, so if you make any suspicious moves, they will likely approach you at a moment’s notice.

National treasure-ranked…! Knowing there are treasures like that nearby has my heart pounding.

Uuu… Please spare me from any rope~

Not to worry. In the room I’ll be guiding you to, there is nothing tricky like that hanging around.

Until the day of the banquet, please do take it easy here as if it is your own home.

It’s gonna be hard to see this place as my own home… Right, Yume?

…What?

“What”? C’mon, at least respond back with a yes, or something. You haven’t been saying a thing this whole time.

You looked a little excited before we got to the mansion, y’know?

…That’s because this is nothing like the mansion that Yume imagined.

This is the home of “Knights”… of a knight, so Yume imagined a castle from a fairytale.

One surrounded by stone walls, and opening the doors would greet you on a long, red carpet…

And the fountain in the courtyard would have little birds flying about as they sing…♪

You’re able to imagine it so vividly, it’s honestly a talent in a way.

Though there is no fountain in my household’s garden, there is a pond. I’ll guide everyone there later.

There’s a pond? Now you’ve got me curious. Aren’t you excited too, Yume?

…Mhm.

(His reactions are so lukewarm… Well, I guess he won’t immediately let down his walls, huh.)

(Sengoku mentioned that he wanted to understand Yume more, but now that Yume’s actually in front of him, it seems that he’s not sure how to approach the topic.)

(I gotta be the one to change up the atmosphere by bringing up the topic!)

Erm, so Anzu-san’s gonna join us at night; is that right, Suou?

Yes. She said she’ll start making the outfits tonight, so she’s been preparing all the fabric and decorations she’ll need in the afternoon.

Got it. But is it really alright for us to sleep over at your house? Isn’t everyone in your home busy with preparing for the banquet…?

There’s no need to worry. You’re helping me, if anything. This way, I can efficiently make contact with the people at home while still mingling and having briefings with everyone.

The only thing is that, I’m afraid to say that I will likely be leaving my seat often. I hope you can understand.

It is alright, there is no need to worry about us. I’d much rather you focus on what you must, Suou-kun~♪

Thank you very much. That being said, I have been working incessantly these past few days, so I hope to spend the time with everyone today as much as possible.

Right then, this is the guest room I’ve prepared for everyone.

Once you’ve taken a breather after settling down your luggage, I’ll guide you through the garden.

Thirty minutes later…

Fwooh…! This is one fine Japanese garden!

This is exactly the epitome of “wabi-sabi”![^] I feel my heart cleansing.

Mheh. I would not even be surprised if Kanzaki-dono appeared before our eyes from the shade of that rock over there~♪

That would definitely be surprising, though…?

A garden like this makes you think of the oniwaban! Though the front seems furnished to be a garden, in reality, it is where the secret corps take on secret orders from the shogun!

Though ninjas are a little different from that, I still find it relatable in a way.

I’m perfectly fine with you doing as you like, but climbing up the garden lanterns is dangerous, so please refrain from anything of the sort.

Why, of course! I am a ninja who is aware of his position.

Even though you’re the same guy who made such a mess over at Shinkai-senpai’s house, you’re now barred from it?

Uuu… Let’s agree not to mention that~!

Ahaha. …Yume imagined a European-looking castle, but…

The garden in Suou’s home kinda reminds me of the one you’d see in a Japanese castle. This is pretty good too, no?

…No. Don’t put them in the same group.

Japanese food and Western food may both be food in the end, but it is entirely different. The same goes for castles.

Ohh. You’ve got your own particulars, huh?

Mashiro-san just generalizes way too much.

Japanese cuisine and Western cuisine… Ah—

Mm? What’s up, Suou?

Ahh, no. I was simply reminded of what my Chef told me.

I thought it would take too much time on both sides to be asked every time, so the Chef is usually in charge of thinking up what cuisine to prepare for the day.

However, they said that they would like to prepare tomorrow’s lunch Course based on our wishes, so could you choose between Japanese cuisine and Western?

Japanese cuisine! (Western cuisine!)

Ehh? Sengoku, you want Japanese food?

Of course! If I’m to dine in a samurai mansion, then I couldn’t possibly have anything but Japanese cuisine!

That’s exactly why we should choose Western cuisine here, though? He’s got his own personal chef! I’m sure they can totally spruce up something amazing! Right, Yume?

Do as you like. Anything is fine as long as it’s filling.

Hmm… We could also go for a blending of the two.

Japanese food! Japanese food! Japanese food~!

Western food! Western food! Western food~!

Fufu. Well, I suppose we have no choice if it’s turning out this way.

You are in the Suou Family’s premises. So I shall have the two of you follow Knights’ method of settlings matters: a duel…♪

Duel?

Chapter 2

…When I heard you say “duel”, I panicked thinking we’d be thrown into some grand battle, but…

What you actually meant was a pillow fight? Or is it that Knights usually settles arguments with pillow fighting?

No, it was simply a figure of speech. The duels in Knights don’t involve crossing swords like the medieval times, either.

To settle the score, we need to establish a means to do so. That would give everyone the most satisfaction, no?

Good point. This sort of game helps us bond further, so I wish Yume was with us, too.

But he refused to join us, saying that princesses would never do something so barbaric.

Is that so… Knights tends to strengthen their unity through duels, so I was hoping deciding on the meal would help close the distance between Shinobu-kun and Yume-kun…

But we mustn’t force him to join us. There is still time until the banquet, so let’s go slow and steady with the matter.

Mashiro-kun, I’ve gathered all the pillows in the mansion; will this suffice?

Yup, it’s more than enough. …By the way, have you ever had a pillow fight before, Suou?

No, I haven’t. Why do you ask?

Mm~ I just got curious. ‘Cause the pillow fights I usually do don’t involve preparing the pillows in advance.

In school trips or training camps, someone will start throwing pillows to mess around, and it just quickly dissolves into a huge fight — That’s how it usually goes.

I see. I’ve heard about pillow-fighting from Leo-san before, so I had hoped to experience it during our school trip…

Ahaha. Our generation’s school trip didn’t really allow for something like that, right~[^]

Alright then, we don’t need someone to do the final calls, so come and join us, Suou.

Huh? I take part, as well?

Yup. You can still have a pillow fight without a judge, anyway.

Plus, you chose pillow-fighting for the duel because you were interested in it, right?

Well, I can’t really… But all the same, this is meant to be a battle between the two of you…

I fully welcome it! The more people, the more fun it is to pillow fight!

Heard that? So come on, join us.

…Very well then. Then what say the three of us enjoy this together?

The rules are as I explained earlier: The last one with the least amount of pillows near him is the winner.

Alright, so we just have to throw every single one we see! Here goes then… Pillow fight, start!

…Wfpgh!?

You left an opening! I’ve made my first strike already!

I shall make excellent use of my ninjutsu and put up a strong fight! Ninja art: Pillow Fight-no-Jutsu~! Shubabababa! ☆

Ow!? You’re going way too fast, Shinobu-kun!

Nishishi~♪ Speed is the key to victory in a time-limited battle!

Dammit~! Now you’ve done it…!

Let us have an alliance, Mashiro-kun!

Ahaha! What a coincidence, I was about to say the same! Three, two, one—!

Ehhh!? Two against one is just foul play~!

It’s only for now. A one-sided match would just be a bore, after all!

And after that… The last one standing will be me!

Fufu, I refuse to lose… Take this! ♪

(…It was always a mystery to me what could ever be fun about causing a mess in a room by throwing pillows…)

(But I see now it is just so easy to get absorbed into the fun of pillow fighting.)

(Innocent laughter as we compete for the win — How incredible for something as simple as that to lighten my heart this much.)

(…Fufu. It has been so long since I’ve been able to have fun with such innocent feelings.)

(I’ve been caught up by my duties as the head of the family for this banquet all this time.)

(However! Though this may be a game, it is still a battle! I will not allow myself to lose here…♪)

It’s time for the counterattack! Prepare yourselves, Mashiro-kun, Shinobu-kun!

Meanwhile, in the room prepared for Anzu…

…Anzu-san, you’re making outfits?

You didn’t expect Yume to come? Because you heard everyone was going to have a pillow fight?

Yume would never do something like that. What would come out of throwing pillows?

It would only exhaust Yume, and Yume would never do something so childish.

…“It’ll be fun, and you’d be able to get along with everyone”?

…………

…More importantly, you’ve stocked up on a lot of late night meals.

Are you planning to stay up the whole night making outfits? Even though there’s still a lot of time before the banquet?

…You want to make it in time for the practice lessons starting the day after tomorrow? Huh…

Say, Anzu-san, why are you so good at sewing?

Yume also makes cosplay outfits, but… It usually doesn’t go the way Yume wanted…

The end result almost always turns out so differently from what Yume imagined inside… and then Yume’s filled with disappointment.

But you’re able to make outfits just as you designed them, right?

Why can you do it, but Yume can’t? That’s just so frustrating.

…Huh. You started sewing idol outfits from your school days, and after you went independent, you brushed up on all your skills?

Yume sews for Yume’s sake, but you sew for others’ sake…

…………

…Can Yume stay here? Yume wants to watch and learn from Anzu-san, so that Yume is also able to make all of Yume’s ideal outfits.

…Like Esu’s exclusive outfit. And Yume’s own outfit, too — Yume wanted to make both of them.

But Yume doesn’t want it all to end on “it’s just impossible”, so… Let Yume learn from you, okay?

…Yume is aware. Staying up light is bad for the skin, so Yume will sleep once it’s time.

On the next morning… Yume will go back to where everyone is.
