🍕 & 🍟 - Midori & Chiaki Story Recommendation List

If you’re interested to learn more about Midori and Chiaki’s relationship, this list will help you! In here you will find the best picks to get started on reading about them.

Notes:
  • ALL stories listed are recommended to read. They are categorized as such:
    • MUST READ; absolute go-to stories for their relationship
    • VITAL CONTEXT; provides context, whether for their individual character or relationship
    • BIG CRUMBS; story contains many interactions between the two
    • TINY CRUMBS; story contains minor interactions between the two
    • NOTE: Some stories are categorized as more than one thing, ex. “vital context” + “big crumbs”
  • Stories are listed in chronological order. It is not required to read the stories in this order; it’s mainly to act as a timeline for the two’s relationship.
  • There’s a quote bot too! More stories will be added in due time, and a list of all brief mentions of their relationship across the stories is in the making!
