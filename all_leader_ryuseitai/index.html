[ES!! Era Year 2] POWER UP! All Leader Ryuseitai (Leader turns)

Officially released on February 9, 2023 Enstars , 1. Era; ES Era , Year 2 , 2. Unit , Ryuseitai , Ryuseitai POWER UP
5.7k ~7 min

Every time Ryuseitai took turns being the leader after the Ryuseitai POWER UP

POWER UP refers to Ryuseitai’s switch from their previous formation to the all-leader system, taking turns as the leader depending on the project!

2023/1/31 Supervillain – Tetora as leader

Around the last chapters of Supervillain, Ryuseitai play as the evil-tainted heroes for the show ‘Supervillains’. Tetora acts as the leader of the supervillain unit called “Ryuseitai-Black (RB)”. Tetora’s efforts throughout the story end on a massive success for the unit RB, and it inspires the idea of a new direction for Ryuseitai itself.

After the decision was reached, Ryuseitai-Black (RB) officially became the first established Ryuseitai under the new all-leader system! The MV, Colors Arise, showcases the change itself, with Tetora as the center and leader.

2023/5/1 JR Tokai – Chiaki as leader

Ryuseitai have a specific leader even for small campaigns! For the Japan Railway Tokai collab, there was a special office outfit included, and only the leaders wore a hat. Chiaki’s the leader for this job!

2023/6/15 Parallel World – Chiaki as leader

To assist in Ra*bits’ plan to show different types of Ra*bits, Ryuseitai act as the “superhero sentai squad” alternate version in Parallel World. Chiaki plays Tomoya, the leader, as the concept has to do with what he loves: hero shows and tokusatsu.

2023/6/30 Tropical – Midori as leader

Midori’s first time as the leader was settled through a ladder lottery in Tropical. The unit name is “Yuruseitai”. He leads the unit that expresses love for yurucharas! Song MV is RELAX PARADISE.

2023/9/30 Starry Symphony -the first light- – Tetora as leader

Tetora is at the start of the list for Ryuseitai, and is in the center of the illustration.

2023/10/10 ES Fireworks Event – Tetora as leader

Ryuseitai have a specific leader even for small campaigns! For the fireworks campaign, Tetora appears in the campaign’s story alongside the other unit leaders of StarPro, and gets to write his wish on the fireworks with them too!

2023/12/27 2023 End of Year Countdown – Shinobu as leader

Ryuseitai have a specific leader even for small campaigns! All characters that appeared for the login are leaders for their unit, and Shinobu was the leader for Ryuseitai!

2024/1/15 ????? – Shinobu as leader

Shinobu’s first leader project is undisclosed, but canonically happened prior to Stella Maris (set in May). This is mentioned in Stella Maris, Prologue.

2024/1/15 Stella Maris – Kanata as leader

Kanata’s first time as leader was only after the juniors got to have their turn, shown in Stella Maris. Kanata personally calls it “fish Ryuseitai”, but it’s officially called “Ryuseitai-C” (C=Cobalt blue). He chose this name to avoid overlapping with the name RB (Ryuseitai Black), since “blue” also starts with B.

He leads the unit of stars who light up the world! Song MV is Bokura no Stella.

2024/3/9 Ryuseitai Album TRIP – Undesignated

It’s mentioned in the MC Talks that Ryuseitai decided on what to do for the album live show together, rather than through one leader. Song MV is NEW DAYBREAK.

2024/4/23 Free Scouts Challenge Campaign for 9th Anniversary – Midori as leader

During the campaign to receive free scouts, the line-up was fixed to these 4 on the campaign page of the lives challenge. Each character is a unit leader from their respective agency.

2024/9/15 ????? – Shinobu as leader

Shinobu was the leader of a project that occurred between Stella Maris and WILDLAND. This is mentioned in WILDLAND, The Tiger Fumbles Around and Searches 2.

2024/9/15 WILDLAND – Tetora as leader

WILDLAND is Tetora’s 5* event, as the leader of a project. This time, he explores Alice in Wonderland, something he initially didn’t have interest in, to learn about its charms and express them through Ryuseitai! Song MV is WILDLAND SURVIVOR.

2024/10/9 Heart Heat Beat (MV release campaign) – Shinobu as leader

For the Live Festival banners of the Season 2 B-songs, the leader was chosen regardless of the card’s high rarity. Shinobu is shown as the banner for Heart Heat Beat! (※ Small exception is with Ra*bits’ banner, that shows the former leader Nazuna)

2024/11/22 DREAM LIVE -9th Tour "Trapezium Orion"- – Tetora as leader

The leaders blow kisses to the audience in a segment. Tetora was the one who did it for Ryuseitai! (Note: I didn’t watch the Dream Live, so I’m going based on reports of the live)

2025/1/9 New Years MC Story – Midori as leader

Midori appears in the last MC talks alongside the rest of the leaders of the other units!

2025/4/24 BRIGHTEST STARS!! MV – Tetora as center/leader

In the BRIGHTEST STARS!! MV, Tetora is shown in the center for Ryuseitai’s moment, at the “brightest star” scene (1:16) (Note: This is mostly considered a center moment, but it can also be seen as leader).

2025/4/24 BRIGHTEST STARS!! MV – Chiaki as center/leader

In the BRIGHTEST STARS!! MV, Chiaki is shown in the center for Ryuseitai’s moment, at the “transformation” scene (1:29) (Note: This is mostly considered a center moment, but it can also be seen as leader).

2025/11/22 Starry Symphony -Superbloom- – Chiaki as leader

Chiaki is at the start of the list for Ryuseitai, and is in the center of the illustration. He was also confirmed the leader in the Goods Ohanashiban series on YouTube!

Note: Starry Symphony is in November, but the illustration reveal was on 8th August, so for archival purposes I’ve placed the turn according to the initial reveal date.

2025/9/10 ES Fireworks Live Stream Finale – Midori as leader

Midori participated in the finale alongside the other unit leaders to give his thanks, as well as spell out a word with sparklers!

2026/3 + 2026/4 DREAM LIVE Tour 10th ALL STARS!! – Tetora as leader

Tetora is at the start of the list for Ryuseitai, and is in the center of the illustration. He was also shown with the other leaders in the merchandise preview.

Note: DREAM LIVE Tour is in March 2026, but the illustration reveal was on 24th October, so for archival purposes I’ve placed the turn according to the initial reveal date.

2025/11/30 Fan ThanX Festival: OVERFLOW! – Chiaki as leader

OVERFLOW! is Chiaki’s 5* event, as the leader of the project for Ryuseitai's Fan ThanX Festival. Song MV is OVERFLOW LIGHT UP!!!!!.
