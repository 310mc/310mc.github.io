A thread of every time Ryuseitai have switched leadership after the Ryuseitai POWER UP !!

The POWER UP refers to Ryuseitai's switch from their previous formation to the new all-leader system, where they take turns being the leader depending on the project!

Note: As of Supervillain and forward, ALL Ryuseitai members are leaders!

🖤 2023/1/31 Supervillain - Tetora as leader 🖤

Unit name is "Ryuseitai-Black (RB)". Supervillain Epilogue is when the all-leader system was decided upon, and the chapters prior to that was the journey illustrating why and how they reached the decision. The MV, Colors Arise, showcases the change itself.
After the decision was reached, Ryuseitai-Black officially became the first established Ryuseitai under the new all-leader system.

❤️ 2023/5/1 JR Tokai - Chiaki as leader ❤️

For the JK Tokai collab, there was a special work and office outfit added. For the office outfit, only the leaders wore a hat. Chiaki was chosen as the leader for this job!

❤️ 2023/6/15 Parallel World - Chiaki as leader ❤️

To assist Rabits in their plan to show different types of Rabits, Ryuseitai act as the "sentai hero squad" alternate version. Chiaki acts as Tomoya, as he's the leader for this project, and the concept has to do with what he loves: hero shows and sentai formations.

💚 2023/6/30 Tropical - Midori as leader 💚

Unit name is "Yuruseitai". Midori's turn to be leader was settled through a lottery. He leads the unit that expresses love for yurucharas! MV is RELAX PARADISE.

🖤 2023/10/10 ES Fireworks Event - Tetora as leader 🖤

For the fireworks campaign, Tetora shows up in the campaign's story alongside the other unit leaders, and gets to write his wish on the fireworks with them too!

💛 2023/12/27 2023 End of Year Countdown - Shinobu as leader 💛

All characters that appeared for the login are leaders for their unit, and Shinobu was the one chosen for Ryuseitai!

💛 2024/1/15 ?? - Shinobu as leader 💛

Shinobu's first time leader project is undisclosed, but canonically happened prior to Stella Maris (set in May). This is mentioned in Stella Maris, Prologue.

💙 2024/1/15 Stella Maris - Kanata as leader 💙

Unit name is "fish Ryuseitai" by Kanata, but officially it's named "Ryuseitai-C" (C is for Cobalt blue). Kanata's first time as leader was only after the juniors got to try out being a leader. He leads the unit of stars who illuminate the whole world! MV is Bokura no Stella.

🌠 2024/3/9 Ryuseitai ALBUM Trip - ALL leaders 🌠

It's mentioned in the MC talks that they decided on what to do for the album live show together, rather than through one leader. MV is NEW DAYBREAK.

💚 2024/4/23 Free Scouts Challenge Campaign for 9th Anniversary - Midori as leader 💚

The line-up was fixed to these 4 on the campaign page. Each character is a unit leader from their respective agency.

💛 2024/1/15 ?? - Shinobu as leader 💛

Shinobu was the leader of a project that occurred between Stella Maris and WILDLAND. This is mentioned in WILDLAND, The Tiger Fumbles Around and Searches 2.

🖤 2024/9/15 WILDLAND - Tetora as leader 🖤

Tetora's 5* event as the leader of his Ryuseitai. This time around, he explores something he has no interest in, Alice in Wonderland, to learn about its charms and express them through Ryuseitai! MV is WILDLAND SURVIVOR.

💛 2024/10/9 Heart Heat Beat (MV release campaign) - Shinobu as leader 💛

For the Live Festival banners of the B-songs, the leader was chosen regardless of the card's high rarity. Shinobu is shown as the banner for Heart Heat Beat! (Small exception is with Rabits' banner, that shows the former leader Nazuna)

💚 2025/01/9 New Years MC Story - Midori as leader 💚

Midori appears in the last MC talks alongside the rest of the leaders of the other units!
