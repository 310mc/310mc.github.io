[TRANSLATION NOTE] Den-O and Gundam Explanation

August 5, 2024
5k ~6 min

—I… I AM TAKAMINE NOWWWWWW!!

Context

In VS★GOURMET, Flood of Customers 3, Chiaki comes up with a plan to overcome his worst enemy: eggplants.

(—Hold on. It’s because it’s me trying to harvest the eggplants that I find it impossible. However, if I were to let Takamine possess me inside…?)

(Takamine is a boy who loves vegetables, suited to don the green of compassion.)

(So if I let him possess me, then wouldn’t even I, the weakest person against eggplants, be capable of fighting against them…!?)

It’s Den-O ♪ Den-O! ♪”

Chiaki's plan may be out of the ordinary, but it's actually a very appropriate reference here, because that is exactly how Kamen Rider Den-O works!

Kamen Rider Den-O

Released in 2017, Kamen Rider Den-O is a little bit unique from other Kamen Riders. Typically, a Kamen Rider has

In Kamen Rider Den-O, the main character, Ryotaro, has the capability to transform into Kamen Rider. But in his early stages as a Kamen Rider, he's still too weak to fight all on his own. Instead, Ryotaro fights as a powerful Kamen Rider through the help of outer beings called “Imagin”.

How does it work?

  • Each Imagin comes in a different color and weapon choice. The main Imagins that fight alongside Ryotaro are Momotaros (red), Urataros (blue), Kintaros (yellow) and Ryutaros (purple).
Ryotaro surrounded by the Imagin that fight by his side.
  • Their physical appearance (seen above) are based on Ryotaro's imagination. Ryotaro loves Japanese folklore, such as the Tale of Momotaro, because he looked up to the main characters as his heroes. Each Imagin took on the form of a character from one of those folktales.

  • When possessing Ryotaro's body, the Imagin have full control over his actions. Ryotaro also changes in appearance a little to match their color and personality.

  • If the Imagin possesses Ryotaro without giving him a heads up in advance, Ryotaro may lose consciousness and not remember anything that happened while he was possessed.

How Ryotaro looks when possessed by one of the Imagins.
  • Only one of the Imagin can possess Ryotaro's body at a time. They can then transform into their respective Rider form when it's time to fight.
The Red Imagin (Momotaros) possessing Ryotaro and transforming into a Kamen Rider
  • BUT, Ryotaro can also take the initiative himself and choose which Imagin to be possessed by when transforming into Kamen Rider. In doing so, he'll take on the designated Imagin's Kamen Rider form, and the Imagin will have control over his body. See this 15 second video for an idea.

  • For the above video, note how Ryotaro's voice changes to the Imagin's as soon as the transformation is complete, indicating he's now possessed by the Imagin as well.

  • While in Kamen Rider transformation, Ryotaro can telepathically communicate with the Imagin. The Imagin has free will to listen to Ryotaro's ideas/wishes or not, and typically, they work together to defeat the monster of the week.

  • Since each Imagin has his own unique weapon, one Imagin can be more advantageous than the other in a fight depending on the monster of the week.

Breakdown of Chiaki's plan

Now, we can link this back to Chiaki's strategy. Let's compare it to how Kamen Rider Den-O works.

Kamen Rider Den-O's typical episodeChiaki's plan
Monster of the week appears. Ryotaro must defeat it to save everyone.Chiaki is faced with eggplants, his worst enemy. Chiaki must harvest them to complete his assistance work.
Ryotaro can transform into Kamen Rider, but he cannot defeat the monster on his own.Chiaki can transform into a hero (Ryusei Red), but he cannot harvest eggplants as himself.
Ryotaro chooses one of the Imagin's Kamen Rider forms to transform into, usually based on what would be advantageous against the monster.Chiaki decides to become Midori to fight against them, because Midori is "a boy who loves all vegetables". In order to do so, Chiaki imagines himself being possessed by Midori, the same way Imagin possess Ryotaro's body.
Once transformed into Kamen Rider, the Imagin takes over Ryotaro's body and fights as Kamen RiderOnce he transforms into Midori, he becomes capable of fighting against eggplants (which leads him to successfully hold an eggplant, as shown in his CG)

Mobile Suit Gundam 000

—I… I AM TAKAMINE NOWWWWWW!!

That’s Gundam. It’s not even Den-O.

Midori's response in Flood of Customers 4 is related to the very last thing Chiaki says. In Japanese, Chiaki says ore ga takamine da.

This is exactly how the main character says one of his most famous lines in Gundam 000, ore ga gandamu da (I am Gundam). See the first 10 seconds of this video.

This is a very famous line in the anime, and is often referenced as a meme in the fandom.

Released in 2007, Mobile Suit Gundam 000 is one of the more well-known Gundam series

Just like Chiaki, Midori's reference is appropriate here. ore ga [something] da isn't a quote you'd hear at all in Kamen Rider Den-O, and most people will immediately think of Gundam 000 as the reference.

Extra

Kamen Rider Den-O is often referenced in Ryuseitai stories! Chiaki usually references catcphrases from it, but Tetora has referenced it before as well.

  • Chiaki referenced Momotaro's (red Imagin) catchphrases: ore sanjou! (I have arrived) and saisho kara saigo made kuraimakkusu da (I'm at a climax from start to end)
  • Tetora referenced Ryutaros' (purple Imagin) catchphrase: taoshite ii? kotae wa kiitenai! (Mind if I defeat you? Not that I'll listen to your answer!)

(SPECULATION ONLY!!) When Chiaki's VS GOURMET bloomed CG appears in the story, Chiaki transforms into Midori to face eggplants once more. There is green shading on the tips of his hair. This is possibly a reference to how possession works in Kamen Rider Den-O: When an Imagin takes over Ryotaro's body, his hair changes a bit in color!
