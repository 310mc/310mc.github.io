Translation Notes

  1. ↑ Yuruchara; yuru meaning “loose,” “laid-back,” + the word “character”. Yuruchara are a type of mascot character, and their defining feature is their peculiar designs, ranging from bizarre-cute to adorkable. They’re well-known for being used all across Japan for PR purposes for companies, prefectures, etc… For more information read here.
