I am an indoors type of ninja…

Thus, I do not feel as though I can handle this level of hot temperature.

(Tell a ghost story)
  • Possibility 1
  • Possibility 2

Uu, it’s scary, it’s scaryyy~…

Please spare me from this ghost story, Anzu-dono!

I will not be able to go to the toilet at night at this rate~

Uu, a chill ran down my spine…

You are good at narrating a ghost story~

This has made the heat get blown out of me! ♪

  • Possibility 1
  • Possibility 2

So cold—!?

Y-You will startle me if you suddenly poke a cold drink on my cheek~

I almost felt my lifespan shorten…

Fwaah, it is so cold, it feels good~♪

I might be able to get through the heat with this~

Anzu-dono, you have my undying gratitude!

Having fun with a big group is a first-time experience for me~♪

Same.
