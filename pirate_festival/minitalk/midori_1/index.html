Pirate Festival – Midori Produce Course: What’s So Good About the Sea?

Officially released on August 1, 2015 Enstars , 4. Character , Midori
2.3k ~3 min
Mini Talk
Point of View
Anzu
Translation

Story
Pirate Festival

1/3

I’m so depressed, I wanna die…

It’s so hot with the sun shining down mercilessly…

And it’s hard to walk on a sandy beach, I just wanna go home already…

(Give sports drink)
  • Possibility 1
  • Possibility 2

Won’t a sports drink just make me thirstier…?

It’s sweet unlike water…

I’ll definitely get the urge to drink water if I drink that right now…

Ahh, I was actually feeling thirsty…♪

Gulps, gulps… Phew, I feel alive again…

I can drink it all? Then I’ll gladly do so~♪

(Give beach sandals)
  • Possibility 1
  • Possibility 2

This is your beach sandals, right…?

I appreciate the thought, but I won’t be able to wear that size…

I’m grateful you offered, though.

You prepare well, don’t you, Senpai…?

I should be able to walk better on the sand with this…

Thank you so much, Anzu-senpai…♪

2/3

Anzu-san, I’ll carry your luggage, if you like…?

I usually carry stuff for my greengrocer work, so I have the arm strength…

I can carry something as light as that just fine, so…

Thank you.
  • Possibility 1
  • Possibility 2

You don’t have to thank me… Here we go…

Woah! This is pretty heavy stuff, huh…?

It’s making me curious what’s inside…

Um, Anzu-senpai… Could you say that again?

Ahh, that smile… It looks exactly like my favorite yuruchara…!

I feel myself this close to hugging you…♪

Are you sure?
  • Possibility 1
  • Possibility 2

I’m the junior here, so please don’t hold back…

I don’t necessarily want you to be shameless like how… Morisawa-senpai is, but…

I’d like to be relied on a little more…

I’m indebted to you for many things after all, Anzu-senpai…

Plus, you’re a woman, so…

I shouldn’t be treating you like you’re a guy…♪

3/3

The seawater is lukewarm because of this hot weather…

I thought I’d feel more refreshed if I swim a little, but I was just betrayed instead…

Uu, I wanna die…

(Guide him to the beach house)
  • Possibility 1
  • Possibility 2

…The AC in the beach house doesn’t work, right?

Your offer sounds really nice, but…

I’d like to rest in an air-conditioned room right now…

I could eat shaved ice at the beach house, right…♪

Which flavor do you like, Anzu-senpai?

I don’t have any particular favorites, so I’ll leave it up to you~♪

(Mention the good things about the sea)
  • Possibility 1
  • Possibility 2

You get to eat fresh seafood…?

I can’t stand fish’s eyes, though…

I don’t really like the bulging stare…

Listening to the sound of the waves calms your heart…?

Ahh, I think I kind of get it…♪

The sound of waves is calming to hear, so you can’t help the urge to listen to it forever, right…
