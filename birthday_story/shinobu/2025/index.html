Birthday Quote

Yaaaay, thank you so much! I hope to grow and mature splendidly, this year more than the last, and next year more than the current! ♪

Before the Birthday Live Stream

It’s the birthday live stream I’ve been eagerly waiting for! I was so excited for this that I woke up much earlier than usual.

In fact, I even had a dream that I was live-streaming. And so, I’ve already rehearsed and everything—…

Huh? There’s some lint on my shoulder…? Wait, you’re exactly right!

You truly saved me there! If you hadn’t told me, Anzu-dono, I would have started my live stream with the lint still there!

This is a special live stream, so I want all my fans to see me dressed up nicely and all grown up! ♪

Birthday Live Stream Start

Transform! Birthday live stream with @Sengoku_Ryusei, start!1

Let’s send Nice!’s to Shinobu and enjoy the live stream!

Welcome to my live stream! I was feeling nervous up until a moment ago, but I felt all of that fade away thanks to everyone’s supportive comments!

Ehehe. I feel like I could respond to any request right now, no matter what comes my way~♪

…Well, that is simply a jest from my over-excitement. However, I still hope to make this a live stream that everyone will be glad they got to experience.

So please go ahead and ask me anything you’ve been curious about!

Erm. Right then, that’s it for greetings. It’s time to start the Q&A segment!

During the Birthday Live Stream 1

"I want to hear about ninjas from you!"

I’ve been seeing some fans comment their anecdotes of making origami shurikens with classmates, or going to the library to learn more about the history of ninjas.

And earlier, I even got a request to hear more about ninjas!

Ehehe. I’m so happy I got to talk about the ninjas I love so much on my birthday~♪

Well, when I saw a comment saying, "Shinobu-kun is so cute when he’s having fun talking!", I was a tinsy little embarrassed, though…

But still! I shall never forget this day for the rest of my life~♪

During the Birthday Live Stream 2

"Your face stickers are so pretty!"

Ah! I’ve received another comment. Let’s see, "Your face stickers are so pretty"?

Oohh, of course you’d notice the face stickers! ♪ The truth is, each color and design are different from one another.

Speaking of which, the colors were immediately decided, but… I couldn’t decide with which design to go with, the hearts or the stars.2

So I asked everyone in Ryuseitai for their thoughts.

And so, by majority vote, I went with the same heart design as Shinkai-dono. I am truly glad that I went with this design in the end!

Q&A Time

Note: Before the idol’s birthday, Enstars official twitter opened a form to gather questions for the birthday idol, and IRL fans could send in one question per idol (Seen here). The following questions were selected from that form.

Sengoku Shinobu has arrived, nin-nin at the ready! ♪ Ehehe. I wanted to have fun with everyone, so I prepared a lot of topics in advance!

Whoops, that’s right! I shall be answering your questions as well, so I await for your comments! ♪

Do you drink milk every day?

Every day!? Well, there is no chance I’d be drinking it that frequently, but… Whenever I have a meal with Tetora-kun, we tend to drink some together.

However, Tetora-kun mentioned that he’s grown taller, likely due to drinking milk…

So it might be best to take up more opportunities to drink milk, in hopes that it makes me taller, too!

What if you got an offer to play the bad guy in a sentai show?

Mm~ I had a feeling I’d get a question of this nature… But now that I’m actually presented with the question, I’m conflicted with what my answer would be.

Either way, I would consult everyone in Ryuseitai first. Ah—! Of course, without getting into the confidential details in any way!

After that, I’d also like to hear out the person who sent me the offer. Such as, why did they choose me? I’d like to think about my decision with their reasons in mind.

What sort of training have you been doing lately?

I’m very delighted that you’d ask! ♪ I’m currently training myself in developing my discernment skills!

Jutsu’s such as the Substitute Jutsu are also important… However, if say, I’m ever surrounded by enemies, I must also decide how to cut my way through at the very same instant.

And so, I have been playing puzzle games during my free time. I read in a book that it is the perfect way to develop your discernment skills.
