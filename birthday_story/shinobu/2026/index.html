Birthday Party Stream

Preparing for the Birthday Party Stream

I’m so thrilled that the both of you agreed to appear as guests for my stream today…! It made me so happy~♪

Of course I would! It’s your birthday, Sengoku! ☆

Yeah. Although… I can see why you’d ask Morisawa, as he’s in your unit, but why did you ask me to join?

The truth is, I had already decided that I’d request this from the first person outside of Ryuseitai who sends me birthday wishes.

And I just so happened to fit that exact criteria, huh? This is a wonderful opportunity for the both of us, so I’ll make sure to fulfill my role as a guest.

I very much appreciate that! I’d love your help in bringing smiles to everyone who’ll be coming to celebrate my birthday~♪

Start of the Birthday Party Stream

“Thank you all for coming to my birthday stream~♪”

“I’m so happy that there’s this many people here to celebrate my birthday!”

“…Ahh, right! I have called over for some special guests today, in hopes of making this more enjoyable to everyone here!”

“Can you guess who those guests may be?”

“Fu fu fu. I will not give the answer just yet, so please look forward to the stream while making your guesses~♪”

During the Birthday Party Stream 1

“Tadaaah~! ☆ The first guest is Hasumi-dono!”

“I’m Hasumi Keito, from Akatsuki. Happy birthday, Sengoku.”

“Yaaay~♪ Did you know? Hasumi-dono sent me a happy birthday message as soon as it became my birthday!”

“I just happened to realize it was your birthday today, that’s all. I didn’t expect you to reply back immediately, though.”

“I was waiting in anticipation, knowing I’d get messages from everyone. There were other people who also sent me birthday wishes afterwards~♪”

“Hm, that’s all because of the type of person you are, Sengoku. I should also learn more from you.”

During the Birthday Party Stream 2

“To everyone watching! Let’s say happy birthday to Sengoku one more time, together!”

“Three, two, one… Sengoku, happy birthday…☆”

“T-This is a little embarrassing, but… I’m very grateful that you’re here today, Morisawa-dono!”

“Of course I’d come rushing to my precious comrade’s birthday! ☆ I’m so happy I get to celebrate your day like this!”

“The comments are overflowing with birthday wishes to you, too. It really shows that you’re loved by so many people, Sengoku.”

“Fufu, that makes me so happy to hear. I’m ever so grateful…♪”

During the Birthday Party Stream 3

“It is about time this live stream comes to an end.”

“I feel a little lonely ending it here, though. Time flies by so fast when you’re having fun.”

“Of course, it goes without saying that I’m grateful to all of you viewers, but I am also just as grateful to our guests, Hasumi-dono and Morisawa-dono. It is thanks to them that this stream ended with lots of fun.”

“Ahh, right! Please do post your thoughts about my quiz on social media!”

“I shall be checking just how well everyone knows me…♪”
