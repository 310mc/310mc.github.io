Birthday Quote

I’m so thankful to have my birthday celebrated by you! I’m itchin’ to open everyone’s presents already, too~♪

Before the Birthday Live Stream

Oh yeah, I wanted to tell you this for a while now, Anzu-anego… Thank you so much for all the efforts you poured into this live stream!

Ahaha, your face looks like it’s saying, "Where did this come from all of a sudden?", I can tell! You don’t usually show your emotions on the outside, but you’re sometimes easy to read~♪

The truth is, Morisawa-senpai told me all ‘bout it. That he spotted you working hard ‘til late at night…

And you even adjusted my schedule, got the set-up for the live stream ready… I know all ‘bout how you’ve prepared so much for me.

That’s why, before the live stream starts, I wanted to properly express my gratitude!

Birthday Live Stream Start

Transform! Birthday live stream with @Nagumo_Ryusei, start!1

Let’s send Nice!’s to Tetora and enjoy the live stream!

I’m grateful to you all comin’ to my live stream! Getting to celebrate my birthday with everyone… This is the best birthday ever~♪

Hehe! I’ll do my best with this live stream so that it turns out to be a special day for everyone as well, so! I’m lookin’ forward to spending time with all of you!

Oh yeah, I’m happy to see so many comments comin’ my way already…

And I plan to respond to as many requests as I can as long as I have the time, so please feel free to send as much as you like~♪

Also, thanks to everyone in Ryuseitai helping me practice answering questions one by one, I feel confident that I can manage this.

I’ll answer any and all questions as manly as possible, without any hesitation, so please look forward to it!

During the Birthday Live Stream 1

"How can I get stronger?"

I’ll read this comment next. Hm, hm… "I’ve decided to take the karate promotion exam"?

Oohh! That’s somethin’ I’d totally want ya to do your best to pass! I’m sure it’s nerve-racking, but go for that challenge while believing in yourself!

Mm~ There’s all sortsa things when it comes to strength, and I’m still nowhere near that level compared to Taishou, but… there’s one thing I can say for sure!

Establish a goal, and work hard training for it every day. If you just keep up the hard work, you’ll surely grow stronger; that’s what I think~♪

During the Birthday Live Stream 2

"I’d like a pep talk."

Somethin’ that’s surprised me is that there’s a lotta requests asking for a pep talk.

…And as soon as I said that, more came in!

Everyone’s reasons are different it seems, like "It’s so I can study for a subject I’m bad at", or "I can’t get myself to confess to my crush, so I’d like a pep talk"…

But I can tell that you’re all tryin’ your hardest to change your current state!

Ossu! If you’re fine with me, then I’d be happy to fulfill that request!

Q&A Time

Note: Before the idol’s birthday, Enstars official twitter opened a form to gather questions for the birthday idol, and IRL fans could send in one question per idol (Seen here). The following questions were selected from that form.

Ossu! Thank you all for coming to my live stream! I’ll be answerin’ questions you’ve sent me, so please look forward to it!

Hehe! It always makes me happy to have my birthday celebrated, no matter how old I get!

Please tell us your muscle-building work-out regime.

Ooh~! I’m so happy there’s someone interested in muscle-building! Aight, I’mma answer that right now~!

I make sure to have push-ups, sit-ups… and also squats to improve my muscle movement throughout my whole body. This is a pretty popular way to train, actually.

I also do some training that uses equipment for box jumpin’! I’d love you all to try it out, too~♪

Do you take after your dad, or your mom?

Mm~ I haven’t really thought ‘bout it much, but now that you mention it… I feel like I’ve heard ‘bout how boys tend to take after their moms…?

Well, no clue if that’s actually true or anythin’, though. There was never a moment when I talked to my friends ‘bout it, either…

Ah! But I’d say I prolly take after my dad when it comes to being an outdoor person. He used to take me fishin’ with him a lot in the past~♪2

Are you planning to get more ear piercings?

Hmm, I can’t say for certain, since who knows what’ll happen in the future… But for the time being, I don’t have any plans to do so.

But the other day, Hiiro-kun told me he saw an ear piercing that would suit me in a store. He said it has a similar feel to the one I own.

He observes me well, huh~ Well, I’m pretty curious what sorta ear piercing it is, and Hiiro-kun’s feelings made me happy, so I’m hopin’ to check out that store sometime!
