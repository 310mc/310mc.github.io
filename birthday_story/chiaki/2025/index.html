Birthday Quote

I’m such a fortunate guy, having my birthday celebrated this much. Responding to everyone’s feelings is just another reason why I have to keep growing further and further! ♪

Before the Birthday Live Stream

Anzu, hear me out. You told me that I can deliver my stream however I like, right?

That’s why I wanted to invite everyone in Ryuseitai to my stream, but they refused…!

Ah, no, it’s nothing upsetting! It sounds like Ryuseitai already made plans to watch over my stream together.

Takamine even told me, "We can’t just butt in and steal the spotlight from the main lead." I can’t really argue against that.

Fufu, they’ve done this much for me. To everyone who’s looking forward to my stream, I’m going to pour in all my efforts to bring smiles to their faces!

Birthday Live Stream Start

Transform! Birthday live stream with @Morisawa_Ryusei, start!1

Let’s send Nice!’s to Chiaki and enjoy the live stream!

To all of you watching this stream, hello! Ryusei Red, Morisawa Chiaki is finally here! ☆

Heroes typically arrive only in the nick of time, but this is a solo live stream! So I made sure to be here ahead of time!

Oh, yeah! As I understand it, everyone in Ryuseitai is also watching this live stream.

I’ve grown a year older, so I can’t be showing any uncool moments here.

I plan to fire up this live stream with all my might, so please enjoy yourselves! ♪

During the Birthday Live Stream 1

"Please do a signature pose!"

Ooh! The next request is, "Please do a signature pose!", huh…!

Hm, hm… I see, so you’d rather I keep my usual catchphrase and signature pose for when I’m with Ryuseitai?

Got it! Get ready, for I’m about to demonstrate a custom version of my signature pose!

During the Birthday Live Stream 2

"I’m so happy I get to see lots of smiles from you."

Fuhaha! Thanks for all the fun comments! Now then, I’d like to head onto the next request…

—!? A wave of comments suddenly started flooding in!?

Ooh, everyone who’s been watching is also promoting my birthday stream on social media, asking people to come celebrate together?2

There are so many people here, using up their time just for me. There’s nothing that could make me happier!

And there’s even comments saying, "I’m so happy I get to see lots of smiles from you." I almost can’t get myself to end the stream at this point…☆

Q&A Time

Note: Before the idol’s birthday, Enstars official twitter opened a form to gather questions for the birthday idol, and IRL fans could send in one question per idol (Seen here). The following questions were selected from that form.

Thank you for sending in so many comments. I can say for sure that your passionate feelings have reached me!

Aaalright, it’s time we start the Q&A segment! I’ll make sure to answer every question with all my power, so look forward to it…♪

What do you like in your miso soup?

I have plenty of things I like in it!3 Onions, potatoes, even asari clam… The best thing about miso soup is almost anything works with it! ♪

It’s actually tricky to nail it down to only one… But if I had to, I suppose I’d go with miso soup that has seasonal fish in it?

I had one at a filming job before, and it was absolutely delicious. The umami of the fish melted into the soup perfectly. Just remembering it makes me want to have another bowl of it again.

Can I become a hero, too?

Of course you can! Everyone holds the qualifications to become a hero, no matter who they are.4

But even then, one can’t themselves a hero and nothing else. Heroes are meant to be everyone’s hope. You have to train yourself physically and mentally to grow strong, so that you don’t give in to evil.

And then, when you rouse to take action for the sake of rescuing someone, that’s when you’ll become a true hero for the first time!

What’s something you’ve done for your parents recently?

Hm… I can’t really say this is anything special, but I’ve been helping them carry things and massaging their shoulders.

Mm? "How about gifting them a ticket to one of Ryuseitai’s live shows?", huh…

They’d be able to see how well I’ve been doing and how much my Ryuseitai comrades and fans have been supporting me… So that might just be a great idea~♪

  1. ↑ All idols have unique account usernames. Ryuseitai share a similar username pattern, "(Last name)_Ryusei"; ryusei meaning “meteor/shooting star”.
  2. ↑ It’s not the first time Chiaki trends on social media in some way. It’s happened in Wedding March! (Epilogue), when he messed up his confession line on TV, and in VS★GOURMET (Flood of Customers 3), when he transformed into Midori on live television. What’s more, the Wedding March! confession line even won the best Ryuseitai phrase of the year in 2023, voted by IRL fans!
  3. ↑ Miso soup is a common topic for Chiaki, and his most prominent one happened in Wedding March! (Epilogue), when he messed up his confession line on TV.
  4. ↑ Only slightly related, but Chiaki’s FS1 5* story, You’re A Hero, Too!, covers this sort of topic too (just as the title implies)!
