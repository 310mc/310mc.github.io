Context

Starting sometime in early July, all ES units will be living together in their own respective room on Mega Sphere (the floating ship above ES). NiceP announced that it will last for a month. During that month, they’ll be live-streaming as part of their job, called the “Mega Stream”.

This storyline will be covered in the first main story of the Mega Sphere arc, “Mega Stream”.

This Mini Talk covers Ryuseitai’s live stream where they introduce themselves.

Thursday Mega Steam; Ryuseitai, Part One

Season: Summer (ES 2nd Year, July)

1/3

Hello. This is Ryuseitai speaking; we’re in charge of the Thursday stream… I’m being told to start with a self-introduction, so I’ll be doing that.

My name is Takamine Midori. My hobbies are collecting yuruchara merchandise, and… DIY, too.

Um, do people get what I mean by “DIY”? It’s basically, well, doing carpentry at home…?

“What sort of things do you make?”

Erm… I make things with practical use. Like shelves, storage boxes, that sort of thing…

It’s nothing fancy, just stuff that are handy to have. I think it’s mostly simple stuff from me.

Also… I’ve made a case to display my yuruchara merchandise before. ♪

“It’s amazing that you can do DIY!”

Ah, there’s already a comment this fast. Ermm… No way. I don’t make anything special, y’know?

I don’t have any of the real tools, either. I just make small stuff like simple shelves and the like…

But… it’s really nice to see things take form into something useful. That’s why I’m kind of into it lately…♪

2/3

hello~ (waves) i am shinkai kanata, from ryuseitai.

my hobby is [water-bathing]. [puka puka] is the best on warm days…♪

where is your favorite spot to [puka puka], everyone? i would love to know.

“Puka puka?”

yes~ [puka puka] is the sound you hear when you are floating in the [water]. like a [pond] or the [sea]…

[puka puka]-ing while the [waves] rock you about puts you in a lovely mood, doesn’t it?

i cannot swim, though, so i would simply just sink to the bottom if i did that in the sea. buku buku, bubbles going up to the surface…♪

“What about swimming?”

people often tell me that this is [surprising] to hear, but… i cannot swim.

i will simply sink to the bottom in any [deep] bodies of water, like the [sea] or the [pool]…

i [love] to splish-splash in the [sea]. it is fun just having my feet in the water~♪

Ossu! I’m Nagumo Tetora, from Ryuseitai!1

My hobby is muscle-building, and my specialty is martial arts! There’s nothing I love more than movin’ my body!

I pour my everyday efforts into my goal of becoming a strong, cool man!

“Who do you admire?”

Ooh, I was just waitin’ for you to ask!

The person I admire is Taishou—Kiryu Kuro-senpai, from Akatsuki!2

I still haven’t gotten anywhere near enough to see his back — But one of these days, I’ll catch up to him, and become a big, kind, and strong man just like Taishou!

“Are you also particular with food?”

I usually eat ready-made meals. If I’m focused on nutrition, then that’s the quickest way to go ‘bout it.

But when I have the time, I try to make food on my own! ♪

I’m studyin’ up on fry pan-based cooking! I hope to become skilled enough to appear on a cooking show someday! ☆

Thursday Mega Steam; Ryuseitai, Part Two

Translation Notes

  1. ↑ Tetora’s first person pronoun is ore (俺), a masculine pronoun. But in the live stream, he instead refers to himself with jibun (自分) in front of the viewers, which is more humble in comparison, but not excessively humble either. Jibun is also used by people who are taiikukai-kei; a description Tetora has used about himself, taiikukai-kei describes those who are part of a sports club.
  2. ↑ Taishou (大将) is how Tetora calls Kuro. It means “chief”, “boss”, “general”.
  3. ↑ When an army needs to flee from a battle, those who are situated at the back are called shingari. Since fleeing is a risky move that exposes the squad’s back to the enemy, the shingari’s role is to defend their allies from the far back until they’ve successfully fled.
