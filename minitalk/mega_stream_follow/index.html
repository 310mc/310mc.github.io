Context

Starting sometime in early July, all ES units will be living together in their own respective room on Mega Sphere (the floating ship above ES). NiceP announced that it will last for a month. During that month, they’ll be live-streaming as part of their job, called the “Mega Stream”.

This storyline will be covered in the first main story of the Mega Sphere arc, “Mega Stream”.

Follow Closely! Ryuseitai, Part One

Season: ES 2nd Year, Summer (July)

1/3

Fuhahahaha! ☆ Looking at the place from this view makes me truly realize how spacious it is!

Mm? I look to be in a great mood? Ooh, so you noticed, Anzu!

The truth is, I just got a reply back from everyone in Ryuseitai. We’re going to have a tour around the Passage Arcade together!1

That’s wonderful.

Yeah. There would be days when we’d each get our own jobs to do, and so we wouldn’t even get to see one another at all.

That made me feel a little lonely, but — We’re going to be in the same room for a while, so!

I’m happy to have many chances to talk to them a lot!

Now I get why.

Yeah! Also, Takamine replied back a lot quicker than usual. It seems like my love has finally reached him!

Today calls for a celebration, I’ll have to make some sekihan…♪2 Or, well, while I am excited enough to have that thought pop into my mind—

There’s a lot of shops in that area, so… It’s probably best that I start figuring out which places we should check out first!

2/3

Sigh, I’m suffering… First I accidentally knock on the door of someone else’s room, then I forget my socks at the laundry area…

Well, Shinobu-kun brought the socks back for me, at least. It’s been a busy time, so I was hoping to shut myself in our room when I don’t have work, but…

Just as I was about to do that, Morisawa-senpai called for all of us, saying he wants to explore the place.

Look at this, it’ll cheer you up.

Eh!? A yuruchara I like has a new video up…!

Uwaah, I didn’t notice at all! Thank you for letting me know.

There’s still some time before all of us have to meet up, so I’ll watch it while I can~♪

Sounds like you’re going.

Well, everyone else in Ryuseitai was on board, and I did want to double-check where things are in the area, so I had no choice, is all.

Plus, even if I hadn’t gone… Morisawa-senpai would’ve been annoying about it.

So I just gave up as soon as I realized today’s just going to have to be like this.

Follow Closely! Ryuseitai, Part Two

1/3

I woke up much earlier than I usually do today.

There’s the saying that "the early bird gets the worm", and it would have been a waste to simply head back to sleep, so in order to go on a walk… Ah, no, in order to head out on an investigation, I snuck out of the room.

However, as I was walking, I heard a sound coming from the laundry area. I thought I was the only one awake, so it surprised me!

What on earth caused that sound…?

Mm! I was curious too, so I went to check what it was. But there was nobody there…

W-Wait, that isn’t what I meant! A basket someone had left there fell down, that is all!

It was all a coincidence, simply a coincidence… It isn’t a scary story at all, really!

Was someone there?

Fufufu, it was Shinkai-dono! I was so certain that he was resting in our room, so I thought I was dreaming for a moment.

Apparently, as he was searching around for a place to puka puka, he wound up at the laundry area.

Since it was precisely the right time for it, I invited Shinkai-dono to go to the big bathhouse together.

2/3

Maaan, you really saved me! If you hadn’t pointed it out, Anzu-anego, I would’ve gone to the sport venue while still lookin’ like that!

Guh, and I checked over my looks so many times before leaving the room, too… I just can’t let my guard down with this bed head!

Well, I promised Hiiro-kun we’d train today, and I doubt he would’ve laughed at my bed head… But it’s important manners to at least keep yourself lookin’ tidy.

It’s not springing up anymore.

Haah, it was even worse when I woke up. It’d spring back up no matter how many times I pushed it down or drenched it with water…

I woke up early and everythin’, but it all went to waste ‘cuz of this!

But the sole saving grace was Shinkai-senpai bein’ really happy ‘bout it, saying, "we are matching~"

It looked quite wild on you.

I’d rather not have the wild look, really. But I guess part of me can’t help but accept that it has that feel, considering it’d just keep springin’ back up no matter how many times I pushed the hair strand down.

Anyway, would you like to come train with us, Anego? ‘Course, it doesn’t have to be the exact same routine we take, but…

It’s apparently a pretty good sport venue, so even a light run should help lift your spirits.

3/3

Ooh, you know about it too, Anzu!? Yeah, yeah, it’s those videos that compile the daily lives of dogs and cats.

During my break time, I watched some videos on a whim when it randomly appeared on my feed, and then it slowly started to put me in a cozy, good mood.

I kept thinking to myself to make this my last video, but before I knew it, so much time had passed before I actually stopped!

I recommend the ones where they’ve just woken up.

The videos where they get surprised by their bed head in the mirror, or let out a sound as if to say good morning are pretty soothing? I see, I’ll check them out later!

Oh yeah, speaking of waking up, I just remembered something… It seems like this morning, Nagumo was battling against his bed head.

My head wasn’t working yet as I had just woken up, so I didn’t notice at the time… But later on, Kanata told me about it while looking to be in a really good mood.

Time passes before you know it with those videos, right?

Mm! Absolutely so. It acts as both a nice way to relax, and a way to look up which streams and videos are popular right now.

There’s nothing I can say to myself right now except I was very naive to think, “It’s the perfect two-birds-with-one-stone!”

However, in terms of camera angles, stream length, pacing, and so on… I got to learn a lot about live-streaming through these videos!
