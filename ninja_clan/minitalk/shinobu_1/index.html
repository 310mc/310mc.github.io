Ninja Clan – Shinobu Mini Talk: Go Go Ninja Training

Officially released on November 30, 2021 Enstars , 4. Character , Shinobu
2.2k ~3 min
Mini Talk
Point of View
Anzu
Translation
310mc
Story
Ninja Clan

1/3

Aaalright, it is time I commence my ninja training for today~!

But first, I must do my warm-ups! One, two, three~☆

Ohh! Anzu-dono! Hello there~♪ (waves)

Oh right…

Hmhm, you’d like to send some documents to the Student Council?

I plan to go to the office after this, so you may leave it in my hands.

I was looking for you.

Mm? Do you have business with me?

Ooh, I see. You have a question about accounting. You can count on me for that!1

What were you doing?

Why, ninja training, of course!

Would you like to join me, Anzu-dono? You are wholly welcome to! ♪

2/3

The way of the ninja also starts from the first step. It is important to train on a daily basis, bit by bit.

I’ve compiled training regimes on a ninja scroll; which one are you interested in, Anzu-dono?

For example, there’s ninja training in which you plant down hemp, and then jump over it every day.

Shuriken training.

Mhm, mhm. Shurikens are absolutely essential to a ninja!

I bought me new shuriken just the other day~ They’re made of rubber so they do not stab into anything, but they can fly far~♪

Wall climbing.

In order to become a ninja, a nimble body is indispensable!

Ahh, but I haven’t been climbing the school walls ever since I joined the Student Council. I would only bring trouble to Isara-dono if I caused an incident.

Kuji.

Rin-hyou-tou-sha-kai-jin-retsu-zai-zen!, correct?

It is used when composing oneself, or when enhancing one’s mind state, so I often recite it before Ibegin training.

3/3

Well then, it is time to resume my training!

…Mm? My phone is ringing. Erm, it’s from Isara-dono?

Ermmm, it seems he has something to speak to me about… I wonder what it could be…?

Maybe it’s Student Council work?

But I believe I shouldn’t have any accounting work for the time being…?

Well, thinking about it won’t get me anywhere, so I shall head on to the Student Council Office straight away.

Maybe about work?

That might be likely, indeed.

Isara-dono is always so busy, so I hope to exert my full efforts for his sake, as well!

Maybe about Ryuseitai?

Hmm, that would likely be about Ryuseitai-N, then…2

However, that would mean he’d contact Tetora-kun instead. Perhaps it’s a topic he can’t speak to Tetora-kun about so easily?
