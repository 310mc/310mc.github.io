Story Context

This story is set right after the epilogue of Dynamic Diamond, which was set on the 4th week of July. The ES idols lost against MELLOW DEAR US, which made Anzu lose confidence in her producing skills. In the epilogue of Dynamic Diamond, Hokuto encounters her at night and tells her to choose him for the next showdown, saying that he will guide her to victory.

In the prologue of Mainstream Megasphere, the story continues from that Hokuto line. But Anzu, still feeling conflicted inside, can’t get herself to choose him.

Hokuto tries to talk it out with her and encourage her, but Hokuto’s words only makes her say “Don’t trust me so much” in tears, and she eventually runs away. Hokuto discusses with Trickstar what had happened, and they all decide to meet with Anzu to try and cheer her up.

They then discover that Anzu is nowhere to be found. That’s when Eichi informs them that she had resigned from her job and went back to the ground. News of her disappearance then spreads fast among the ES idols, distraught at the news.

As of this moment, it is not clear why Anzu left Mega Sphere, with most of the characters speculating the reason out of worry.
