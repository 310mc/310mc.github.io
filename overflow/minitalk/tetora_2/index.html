OVERFLOW! – Tetora Mini Talk: Choosing A Present

Officially released on November 30, 2025 Enstars , 4. Character , Tetora
2.4k ~3 min
Mini Talk
Point of View
Anzu
Translation
310mc
Story
OVERFLOW!

1/3

Ohh, so this is a new one too, huh~ It looks like it’s made with a good balance of ingredients~ And as for this one…

Ah! Anego! Hello, thank you for your hard work!

This protein catalogue? Taishou gave me refreshments the other day, so I’m pickin’ out somethin’ to give him back.

That’s so admirable of you.

Heheh, feels embarrassing bein’ told that.

But Taishou is way more admirable and kind, y’know!

Even after he graduated, he’s still always concerned ‘bout me… I can never thank him enough.

I thought it was for yourself.

Yeah, I also stock up for myself! You gotta have these if you work out every day, after all.

But this one’s gonna be for Taishou. Lookin’ at all the options makes me wanna get some for myself, but I’ll hold it in for now.

Since this is gonna be a special gift, I wanna choose a flavor of drink Taishou hasn’t tried before, but I can’t decide at all…

2/3

By the way, how does kinako yoghurt flavor sound…?

Accordin’ to the reviews, it’s not that sweet, so it’s easy to drink, but I haven’t actually tried it myself.

Maybe it’s too risky goin’ with this flavor for protein shake…?

I think it sounds nice.

Hearin’ that from you gives me relief, Anego!

Plus, I feel like Taishou wouldn’t hate a flavor like this.

…Aight, I’ll note it as one option I might take! Thanks for the advice!

Maybe a more standard flavor is better.

You’re right, you can’t go wrong with typical flavors like chocolate.

But I wanna surprise him with a flavor he hasn’t tried before, too…

Y’know what, I’ll buy both the standard flavor and risky flavor, and have him try both!

3/3

Thanks to you, Anego, I feel like I got to choose the right present~♪

By the way, how do you usually go ‘bout choosing repayment gifts for refreshments you receive?

Although, since it’s you, I feel like you easily manage to choose somethin’ for people…

I think over it a lot.

I guess everyone struggles to choose presents for people, huh?

Plus, knowing that all the time I spend pondering is also the same amount of time I’m ponderin’ over the person I’m choosing a gift for… makes even the thinking process a lotta fun for me!

It’d make me so happy if Taishou enjoys the repayment gift I got him, even if only a li’l~♪

You’re right, I don’t think it takes me long to decide.

“When I’m not sure what to get, I ask the person directly first,” huh… That makes sense, that way you can’t go wrong, yeah?

But when you want to have it as a surprise, you double-check the person’s tastes or wants beforehand, huh?

I’m learnin’ so much! I’ll also try to keep everyone’s tastes in mind in my daily life more often, not just Taishou’s!
