OVERFLOW! – Shinobu Mini Talk: Flawless Preparations

Officially released on November 30, 2025 Enstars , 4. Character , Shinobu
2.2k ~3 min
Mini Talk
Point of View
Anzu
Translation
310mc
Story
OVERFLOW!

1/3

Whew, I wasn’t sure what I was going to do for a moment there.

Not only did I drop my shopping bag due to how heavy it was, but everything spilled out of it as well…

Once again, thank you so much for helping me pick them back up!

You’re welcome.

You truly are very kind, Anzu-dono.

After all, you picked up the items even without the knowledge that they belonged to me at first, correct?

That part of you is very much like a hero in a way~♪

That surprised me.

Ahaha, it would be surprising to suddenly see shampoo and soap rolling on the pavement, yes.

I caused troubled to you due to my carelessness, and I feel bad for it.

This is another reminder that I shouldn’t buy too much at once. I’ll carve that note inside of me!

2/3

You’re curious to know what I stocked up so much of?

I was thoroughly preparing myself for the tour we will have for the Fan ThanX Festival. Since it’s a special occasion, I thought to tackle this with a flawless set-up.

Now I only need to prepare for my physical health… What must I be attentive of to keep myself fully energized on the day of the tour?

Proper nutrition is important.

That makes sense, since food is directly connected to our health.

It might be a good idea to think up a food plan for the coming days until the tour, to keep myself properly nourished.

It would greatly help me to hear your thoughts on what would make for good meals, Anzu-dono.

Resting properly is important.

You’re right, I doubt recklessly working hard would be good for my health.

I haven’t been able to take the time for myself due to practice lessons and preparations…

But I shall find the spare time to take breaks, just as you advise, Anzu-dono.

3/3

I am absolutely grateful for you lending me an ear, Anzu-dono.

Ah, I know! Please let me give you a present of some sort.

Here, this is a shuriken-shaped bath bomb! I heard that if you’re lucky, a tiny figurine may appear from inside~♪

What kind of figurine?

Apparently, it would be a ninja-themed figurine, such as a shuriken or katana model.

Among them is the rarest of them all; the golden shuriken~♪

Although, such a color would make it the opposite of hidden. But still, it’s human nature to want something that is called rare.

If it appeared…

If a figurine appeared, you would give it to me?

I appreciate the thought, but I gave that bath bomb to you, Anzu-dono.

If you struck gold, I would be much happier if you keep it for yourself~♪
