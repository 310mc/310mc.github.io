fufufu~♪

Translation Notes

  1. ↑ Referring to Ryuseitai’s state back when Chiaki was a 1st and 2nd year, which is explicitly shown in Meteor Impact. Jin has also previously mentioned that the unit was in an awful state back when he was a Yumenosaki student as well, in Legendary Hero!.
  2. ↑ As a fun note, Chiaki is easily startled by people approaching him; the most notable moments are in Astraea’s Atelier, but it’s happened in other stories like Witchcraft, and even back in his 2nd year of Yumenosaki, Meteor Impact, 2nd part Chapter 2.
  3. ↑ An idiom that refers to someone whose disposition/personality never changes from when they were a young child, even after they’ve grown older. The English version of this idiom is “the child is father to the man”.
  4. ↑ Puka puka means “floating” (Sometimes translated as “bubble, bubble”). As mentioned, when Kanata says this, it’s to do with his favorite hobby, water-bathing, where he “floats” in a body of water.
  5. ↑ As a fun note, Kanata tends to do his own original touch to many things; a notable one is his Ryusei Blue catchphrase (“i have come from the blue sea”). See more in the notes for Yumenosaki Ryuseitai Song.
