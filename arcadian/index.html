What about you, Yuzuru? Did your laundry… Or more like, did your hangers get taken again?

Yeah, I also haven’t seen any today. I don’t get the sense they’re around anymore, even if we checked the area.

Was the hawk so scary that they took just the ripened ones and flew away?

No. To crows, an area that has hawks would be a matter of life or death.

I think it’s unlikely that it would come for food in a place that it wouldn’t want to be in for even a moment.

This is an urban area, not somewhere near the mountains, so I believe there would be plenty of other places for them to find food.

Then, does that mean the one behind my mini tomatoes disappearing isn’t a crow…?

I believe that might be the case.

It wasn’t a crow!? Then who the heck was behind it?

You know as much as I do… I haven’t got a clue, either…

However, there might be a way to pin down the culprit. Fortunately, there seems to be a few tomatoes ready to ripen by tomorrow.

Everyone, please listen to what I have planned.

The next morning, in Seisoukan Dorm’s Courtyard once more…

Yawns, morning~… How’s it looking? Are the mini tomatoes still there?

Yes. They were still there when I checked in the early morning.

Up until now, the culprit has been eating only the ripened ones every time. That means they should come to eat it today, too.

If we stay hidden here, the culprit should show themselves soon enough.

But who could be behind it? A cat wouldn’t eat tomatoes.

Could it be a dog, then?

If a wild dog slipped in here, that’s a much bigger problem than crows in my opinion. That, or it’s very likely a different kind of bird.

Mm~ I feel like no bird would try to come near here now that a hawk flew around the area~

I mean, the hawk feels like the king of birds, and wouldn’t any bird be scared of it?

Huh? Someone’s come over…

That’s… Hiiro-sama, isn’t it?

Oh yeah, Hiiro-chan’s mentioned before that he usually works out in the morning, so that must be what he’s doing today.

—Ahh!? He just ate one of the tomatoes!?

Woah!? That surprised me…!

Midori-kun, everyone, good morning. What are you all doing behind the bushes? You let out a loud yell all of a sudden, so it startled me.

I can’t believe Amagi-kun was the tomato thief all along…! That’s so awful of you!

Eh? Thief…? What on earth do you mean? I swear to God that I didn’t do any misdeed like that! Please believe me!

Ermm… That’s gonna be hard to do when you were caught red-handed, y’know?

We are talking about the mini tomato you just ate, Hiiro-sama. Those tomatoes were being looked after by Takamine-sama.

Ehhh!? These weren’t wild plants!? I’m sorry! I genuinely thought they grew on their own!

Ahh… So Hiiro-chan thought these grew naturally.

Well, I can see how you’d think they’re wild plants, since they were growing in the corner of the courtyard. There isn’t a name plate on them either or anything.

Which means… Amagi was accidentally stealing them ‘cause he got the wrong idea?

Mhm. I’m of course aware that I shouldn’t steal anything from someone’s vegetable field.

But I thought these mini tomatoes were wild plants… I didn’t think they’d be Midori-kun’s.

I’m sorry, Midori-kun.

Ah, no… I know now that it’s just a misunderstanding, and I’m the one who didn’t plant it in a way that makes it clear that it belongs to me. I’m sorry for the confusion.

Uuu… This was such an unexpected conclusion that I just feel exhausted now…

Fufu. It took twists and turns until we reached the truth, but it seems the case is now closed.

Yes… I’m sorry. I caused such a big fuss…

There’s the saying that “all’s well that ends well”, and both Takamine-sama and Hiiro-sama got to clear up misunderstandings between each other, so I would say this is a good conclusion to end on.

Ah, speaking of ending… Could I have that crow picture you drew, Master Artist Fushimi?

Eh? …Well, I was only going to dispose of it, so I suppose I don’t mind.

Yaaay! ♪ I got off to a really bad start, but if I get to have Master Artist’s latest drawing, then this really might just be an “all’s well that ends well” ending after all…♪
