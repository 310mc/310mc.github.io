3k ~4 min
Mini Talk
Point of View
Anzu
Translation
310mc
Story
Christmas Live

1/3

I feel like lately, Nagumo’s attitude towards me is just getting drier…

Compared to me, Anzu’s pretty adored by Nagumo, huh…

At this point, he mentions you almost just as much as he mentions Kiryu! ☆

I wonder why?
  • Possibility 1
  • Possibility 2

I could easily answer that, but I’d rather you notice it by yourself!

I’m sure that’d make Nagumo happy too, so~?

In other words, please give it a thought on your own! ☆

That’s because he adores you as an "older sister", you see!

After all, he typically calls you "Anzu-Anego"!

Please keep being on good terms with Nagumo, Anzu!

He’s just hiding his embarrassment.
  • Possibility 1
  • Possibility 2

It doesn’t seem to be him hiding his embarrassment to me, though…?

Well, no matter. Nagumo’s a boy too, so I’m sure he wants to stand on his own two feet.

Seeing it that way makes even that attitude of his quite heartwarming! ☆

He speaks rather bluntly for something like "hiding his embarrassment", though…?

Even when I ask him for something, he just looks at me with an "Ehh~" look.

I don’t think he hates me or anything, but I don’t feel as though he adores me, either.

2/3

Anzu, thank you for fetching us a job…☆

We’re going to participate in StarFes too, of course.

So this Eve’s Eve Live is the perfect rehearsal for us!

It’s hard to say, isn’t it?
  • Possibility 1
  • Possibility 2

Mm? Do you mean “Eve’s Eve Live”?

Yeah, I also thought it’s a little hard to say.

But it’s already been advertised, so we can’t change it this late in.

True, I feel like I could accidentally fumble saying the name…!

But well, it’s a live show performed before Christmas Eve, so “Eve’s Eve Live” makes sense.

It may be a basic name, but I like how it’s easy to understand! ☆

How’s preparations going?
  • Possibility 1
  • Possibility 2

Mm, we’re moving pretty smoothly without any problems for now.

That being said, problems could still occur even when things are moving smoothly.

So we’ll have to brace ourselves properly and take the plunge with the live show! ☆

Mm, Nagumo brought over Santa-looking outfits for us.

We didn’t have time to prepare, so I was actually thinking to perform with the Ryuseitai uniforms at first, but…

Thanks to these Santa-looking outfits, we can liven up the place in a Christmas Eve-like way! ☆

3/3

That Nagumo… Mhm, he’s truly grown big! ☆

Ohh, Anzu, you heard me talking to myself.

Well, no matter! Please keep watching over Nagumo as he grows!

Do you have a fever by any chance?
  • Possibility 1
  • Possibility 2

Ha ha ha! Nagumo said something similar to me!

You and Nagumo may be similar in a way.

Or rather, maybe Nagumo became more similar to you…?

An ally of justice never catches a cold…!

Well, that’s what I’d like to say here, but you nursed me in the past, so…1

The cold weather is getting harsher lately, so make sure not to catch a cold either, alright?

Err…?
  • Possibility 1
  • Possibility 2

This isn’t the moment to tilt your head; you need to reply back energetically, don’t you?

Nagumo changed not just because of Kiryu, but because of you, too.

Don’t forget that, okay? ☆

Ha ha ha! You won’t know what I mean if I just say it out of nowhere, huh?

You see, Nagumo grew splendidly, even though I had first thought he was a worrisome kid.

And when I realized that that’s also because of you, Anzu, I felt a little emotional! ☆
